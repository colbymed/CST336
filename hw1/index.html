Data Structures

Data structures are an extremely important part of computer science.
Storing and accessing data is a necessity when it comes to making programs, and it has to be done efficiently.
There are many data structures out there, and each have their strengths and weaknesses.
Choosing the right structure can mean the difference between blazing-fast speeds and watching a progress bar move slower than molasses.
